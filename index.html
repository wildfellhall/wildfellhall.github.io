About Me

I love all things language-related. It's what led me to writing, coding, and reading (as well as my username, which comes from my favorite Bronte novel, The Tenant of Wildfell Hall). As one may guess, it also led me to become interested in the training and creation of LLMs, Neural Networks, CNNs, and other such models.

In addition to AI, writing, and reading, I'm a teenage girl who enjoys monochrome movies (my favorite as of now is Bringing Up Baby) and math.
